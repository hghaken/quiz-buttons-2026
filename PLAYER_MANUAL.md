# Quiz Button — Player Manual

---

## Your Button

Every player has a wireless quiz button with:

- A large **push button** on top
- An **RGB LED** that shows the status of your button
- A **buzzer** that gives audio feedback at key moments
- A **rechargeable battery** (USB-C connector)

---

## LED Colors

The color of the LED tells you exactly what is happening.

| Color | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 🟢 **Green** | Button is active — you can buzz! |
| 🔴 **Red** | Button is disabled — wait |
| 🔵 **Blue** | You were the **first** to press |
| 🟣 **Purple** | You were the **second** to press |
| 🟡 **Yellow** | You were the **third** to press |
| ⚪ **White** | You pressed too, but later (4th or beyond) |

> **Tip:** The earlier you press, the earlier you get to answer!

---

## Buzzer Signals

The buzzer inside the button gives you audio feedback:

| Signal | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **1 short beep** | Your buzz has been registered |
| **2 beeps** | The round was reset — button is active again |
| **3 beeps** | Your button has been disabled (wrong answer) |

---

## How Does the Game Work?

### Step 1 — Wait for the question
The quizmaster reads the question. Your LED is **red** — do not press yet.

### Step 2 — Button turns green!
As soon as the LED turns **green**, you may buzz in. Know the answer? **Press the button!**

### Step 3 — Buzz registered
You hear **1 beep** and your LED shows your rank color:
- Blue = you were 1st, Purple = 2nd, Yellow = 3rd, White = 4th or later

### Step 4 — Answer the question
The **first** player (blue LED) gives their answer.

**Answer correct?** → The quizmaster awards points. The question is done.

**Answer wrong?** → Your button turns red (3 beeps). The **next** player in line gets to answer.

### Step 5 — Nobody knows it
If nobody answers correctly, the quizmaster resets the round. You hear **2 beeps** and your LED turns green again — all buttons are ready for the next question.

> **Note:** Players who gave a wrong answer stay red for that question, but can buzz in again on the next question.

---

## Points

The quizmaster awards **1**, **2** or **3** points per question.
The number of points depends on the difficulty of the question.

Your score is shown live on the scoreboard.

---

## The Joker 🃏

Every player has **one joker** for the entire game.

- The joker **doubles all your points** for an entire round
- You can only use the joker **before the first question** of a round
- Use it wisely — pick the round where you think you will score best!

> **Example:** Using your joker in the music round when you know a lot about music = extra reward for every correct answer in that round.

---

## Startup Sequence

When the button is switched on it runs through a short sequence:

| LED Color | What is happening |
|---|---|
| 🟢 Green | Button is starting up |
| 🔴 Red | Connected to WiFi |
| ⚪ White | Time is being synchronized |
| 🔵 Blue + long beep | Connected to the quiz server — ready! |

The button is ready to use as soon as you hear the long beep.

---

## Tips

- **Only press if you know the answer** — a wrong answer blocks you and gives others a chance.
- **Wait for green** — pressing too early does nothing (button is still red).
- **Joker timing matters** — use it in the round where you are strongest.
- **Button disconnected?** Ask the quizmaster to click "Find all buttons". Your button will automatically reconnect.

---

## Charging

- Charge the button using the **USB-C** connector.
- The battery is large enough for multiple game sessions — but charge it after use.
- Do **not** charge while the button is actively in a game.
